Patrol your wiki!

This is a tool for keeping track of new pages in MediaWiki wikis. It visits all of the recent additions to a wiki, ignoring redirects and subpages, and scores them on things like how many categories they have, how many figures and references, and how long they are. The maximum score is 20.

Once you have some results, you decide what to do with the worst pages: visit them, edit them (if you're logged in to the wiki), or tag them with a 'quality' tag.

If you have ideas for new features, please send them along to matt@agilegeoscience.com
